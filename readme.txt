== Description ==

WooCommerce Related Product Slider is an amazing related products slider plugin that will help you to replace the default WooCommerce related products with a slider of all related products. The slider is very simple & easy to use, just active and it’s ready to show the slider of related products in product single page. From the WRPS settings, you can manage the slider settings and styling.

[Live Demo](https://wplimb.com/demo/product/long-sleeve/)

<h4>Features</h4>
<ul>
	<li>100% Responsive.</li>
	<li>Minimalist & lightweight.</li>
	<li>Set number of total products to the slider.</li>
	<li>Change the related slider title & color.</li>
	<li>Change navigation arrow & background color.</li>
	<li>Set the number of product columns into the slider.</li>
	<li>Compatible with any theme.</li>
	<li>Supports all modern browsers.</li>
	<li>And much more...</li>
</ul>
